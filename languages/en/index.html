me

Hi, I'm Juan Antonio García Muelas.

I have always been linked to everything that could bring out my most creative side, starting from small to draw, disassemble and assemble with great curiosity all kinds of objects, o help as much as possible my elders in their work.

All of that first led me to go through Delineation, or work in the world of construction, seeing how the drawing became a visitable or habitable reality.

Then I was able to enjoy everything that surrounds decoration, contract and its facilities commercial, until the arrival of the last? crisis.

You can see much more details in my extensive resume.

That time, so convulsive and problematic for many, helped me to reflect and reorient my education, returning to formal training to learn more about the most current design concept: the web.

After finishing my studies as Senior Technician in Web Application Development just before the start of the 2020 pandemic, I took the opportunity to set up my first projects and to complement what I had learned, with a new training cycle of Senior Technician in Development of cross-platform Applications and, following the modernisation of professional training in Spain, the Professional Master's Degree in Artificial Intelligence and Big Data.

For some, it may seem like the end of the road, but for me, it is the continuation of it, but for a more exciting and ever-changing path.

Don't hesitate to know more about me or meet some of my main proyects.

I have solved the problem, now I must write the code.
